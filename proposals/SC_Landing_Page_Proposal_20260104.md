## 2. Industry Pain Points & Solutions

### Pain Point 1: The "Static Map" Problem
**Challenge:** Customers stand in front of complex floor maps, confused. Digital signage is static and hard to search.
**GBase Solution:** **Map Canvas Technology.**
*   *How it works Check:* Just upload an image of your floor map. GBase automatically identifies shop locations and paths.
*   *Benefit:* Users can click a shop or ask "Where is the Ramen shop?" and get visual + text guidance instantly.

### Pain Point 2: The Inbound Tourism Surge
**Challenge:** Staff cannot speak English, Chinese, Korean, Thai, etc. fluently. Hiring multilingual staff is expensive and hard.
**GBase Solution:** **10-Language Real-time Translation.**
*   *How it works Check:* Japanese FAQs are automatically translated into 10 languages (En, Cn, Kr, Vn, Th, etc.).
*   *Benefit:* One Japanese staff member manages the knowledge base; the AI serves the world. Standardizes service quality across all languages.

### Pain Point 3: High Turnover & Training Costs
**Challenge:** Info desk staff turnover is high. Training them on every new shop or event takes time.
**GBase Solution:** **RAG Knowledge Base (No-Code).**
*   *How it works Check:* Upload tenant manuals, event PDFs, or just the official website URL. The AI learns instantly.
*   *Benefit:* "update once, know everywhere." New seasonal events are answered correctly significantly faster than retraining human staff.

## 3. Key Features for Shopping Centers

### 🗺️ Map Canvas (The "Killer Feature")
Don't just text back. **Show them.**
GBase Support doesn't just read text; it understands your layout. When a user asks "Where is the restroom?", the AI highlights it on your actual floor map image.

### 🗣️ Digital Human Kiosk
**Make it personal.**
Deploy a friendly, animated 3D avatar on your mall kiosks. It speaks, listens (Voice Agent), and bows, creating a welcoming "Omotenashi" experience without human fatigue.

### 📱 LINE Integration
**Support that goes home with them.**
Users scan a QR code to start. They get guidance while shopping, and you get a channel to push coupons or announcements later. (Integrated with standard LINE Bot API).

### ⚡ Real-Time Voice Interaction (New in v3.4)
**Hands-free help.**
For busy shoppers carrying bags, voice interaction is key. GBase's low-latency voice mode allows natural conversation without typing.
